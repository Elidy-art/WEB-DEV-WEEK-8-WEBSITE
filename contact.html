Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn and in their original condition with all tags attached.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order through your account dashboard.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

How do I care for thrifted items?

Each item comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend gentle washing and air drying for most vintage pieces.
